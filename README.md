# Waifu Roulette Lounge

*A refined 1980s city pop anime pixel casino experience*

---

## About

Waifu Roulette Lounge is a stylish virtual roulette game featuring a unique city pop aesthetic inspired by late-night Tokyo rooftop casinos. Place your bets, spin the wheel, and chase big wins—all with simulated currency.

---

## Features

- **Classic Roulette Gameplay** — Straight bets, split bets, dozens, red/black, odd/even
- **Progression System** — Earn XP, level up, unlock cosmetics
- **Multiple Table Skins** — Unlock new visual themes as you progress
- **Dealer Personalities** — Choose your vibe with different dealer styles
- **Run Mode** — 10-spin sessions with grade tracking
- **Persistent Progress** — Your XP, level, and unlocks are saved locally
- **Responsive Design** — Play on desktop or mobile

---

## How to Play

1. **Place Bets** — Click on the table to place chips. Select chip denominations at the bottom.
2. **Spin** — Hit SPIN to spin the wheel.
3. **Win** — Winning bets pay out based on standard roulette odds.
4. **Progress** — Earn XP with every spin. Level up to unlock new cosmetics.
5. **Run Mode** — Challenge yourself with 10-spin runs and earn grades (S/A/B/C/D/F).
